When Jesus Called Her Daughter

Part Two

Hope Begins to Rise

For twelve long years, suffering had become the rhythm of her life. Every physician had offered another promise. Every remedy carried another hope. Every disappointment left another scar. By the time she reached the end of her own strength, she had exhausted every earthly solution and spent everything she possessed in search of healing.

Yet God often begins His greatest work where human ability reaches its end. What no physician could accomplish, Christ would complete in a single moment. Before healing came, however, hope had to awaken. The miracle did not begin with the touch of His garment. It began with the reports that reached her ears and stirred faith within a weary heart.

This section follows the turning point of her story. The silence of twelve years begins to give way to expectation. Despair slowly surrenders to hope. The woman who had spent years searching for answers now finds herself moving toward the One who is the answer.

Every step that follows is a testimony that faith often begins long before miracles appear.
