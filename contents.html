When Jesus Called Her Daughter

Contents

A Journey of Faith, Healing, and Restoration

For twelve long years, one unnamed woman carried a burden that no physician could heal. Her story occupies only a few verses in the Gospels, yet her encounter with Jesus Christ has encouraged believers for nearly two thousand years. This book follows her journey from suffering and isolation to healing, restoration, and the life changing moment when Jesus called her "Daughter."

Each section builds upon the previous one, moving from historical background to biblical insight, and finally to the greater message of redemption found in Jesus Christ.
