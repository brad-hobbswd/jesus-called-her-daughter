About the Author

Bradley Hobbs

Writing to Point Hearts Toward Jesus Christ

Bradley Hobbs is a Christian author whose passion is helping readers encounter the depth, beauty, and life changing power of God's Word. His writing combines biblical history, cultural context, and careful study with a pastoral heart, inviting readers to see familiar passages of Scripture through fresh eyes while remaining centered upon Jesus Christ.

When Jesus Called Her Daughter grew from a desire to look beyond the miracle itself and explore the remarkable story of the woman with the issue of blood. Although her account occupies only a few verses in the Gospels, her journey reveals profound truths about faith, suffering, holiness, redemption, and the compassionate heart of the Savior.

Throughout this book, Bradley seeks to remind readers that Scripture is more than a collection of historical events. It is God's living Word, revealing His character and inviting every generation to place its confidence in Jesus Christ. The same Savior who stopped for one forgotten woman still sees every hurting heart today.

His prayer is that every reader will leave this book with a greater appreciation for the grace of God, a deeper understanding of the Scriptures, and renewed confidence that Christ continues to restore lives through His redeeming love.

Author's Prayer

"My prayer is that as you turn these pages, you will not simply learn more about one woman from Scripture, but that you will come to know Jesus more deeply. May your faith be strengthened, your hope renewed, and your heart continually drawn closer to the Savior who still calls sons and daughters into His family."
