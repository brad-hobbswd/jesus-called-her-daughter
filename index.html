The Story Behind One of the Most Beautiful Words Jesus Ever Spoke

For twelve long years she suffered in silence. She had exhausted every physician, spent everything she possessed, and watched hope slowly disappear. Then one encounter with Jesus Christ changed everything.

When Jesus Called Her Daughter invites readers to journey beyond the miracle itself into the history, culture, and profound spiritual truths surrounding one of the most beloved accounts in the Gospels. More than a story of physical healing, it is a story of restored identity, unwavering faith, and the redeeming compassion of Christ.

Walk through the pages of Scripture as the life of an unnamed woman becomes a timeless testimony that still speaks to broken hearts today.
